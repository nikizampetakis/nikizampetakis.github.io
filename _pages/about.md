I am a third-year PhD candidate in the Department of Economics at Sciences Po. I specialize in Industrial Organization and Antitrust/Competition Economics.<br>
My research examines collusion and its interaction with market structure, with a focus on vertical contracting and auction design. I study how cartels interact with downstream firms and how policy and market design can be used to deter collusive behavior.
